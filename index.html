Projects

Bronco Books for IOS and Android

Santa Clara University Capstone Project completed with Vineet Joshi and Terry Shih. Bronco Books is a mobile Textbook E-Commerce platform for Santa Clara University students to buy and sell textbooks. This project was written in Java and Swift, and made use of Firebase, a "backend as a service", in order to handle authentication, image storage, and our realtime NoSQL database. Firebase was also used for easy access to ML Kit, a mobile SDK that provided access to barcode scanning. This project earned our team The School of Engineering 49th Annual Senior Design Conference Design Presentation Award.
